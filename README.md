# FNP_Sales_Analysis_ExcelProject
Executive Summary – Sales Analysis Dashboard
Overview

This Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by analyzing revenue, orders, delivery efficiency, customer occasions, product categories, cities, and order timing. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor KPIs, identify high-performing areas, and make data-driven decisions for improving sales and operational efficiency.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Revenue
The business generated a total revenue of ₹3,520,984.
Indicates strong overall sales performance across products and occasions.
Average Revenue
Average revenue per order stands at ₹3,521.
Shows healthy transaction value and customer spending behavior.
Total Orders
Total number of orders recorded: 1000.
Demonstrates consistent customer engagement and sales activity.
Average Delivery Time
Average order and delivery time is 5.53 days.
Delivery performance is relatively stable but can still be optimized for better customer satisfaction.
Revenue Analysis
Revenue by Occasion

The dashboard analyzes revenue generated during different occasions and festivals.

Key Insights
Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan generated the highest revenue.
Diwali and Valentine’s Day showed comparatively lower revenue contribution.
Occasion-based sales indicate strong seasonal buying patterns.
Business Impact
Festival campaigns and targeted marketing during high-performing occasions can significantly increase revenue.
Low-performing occasions can be improved through discounts and promotional bundles.
Revenue by Month

Monthly revenue trends highlight fluctuations in sales throughout the year.

Key Insights
Revenue peaks were observed during February and August.
Some months experienced comparatively lower sales activity.
Revenue trends indicate seasonality and customer demand variation.
Business Impact
Businesses can plan inventory and marketing budgets according to peak months.
Off-season months require customer engagement strategies to maintain sales consistency.
Revenue by Category

This section compares revenue generated across product categories.

Key Insights
Colors category generated the highest revenue.
Soft Toys and Sweets also contributed significantly.
Categories like Cake and Plants generated moderate revenue.
Business Impact
High-performing categories should receive higher inventory allocation and promotional focus.
Underperforming categories may require pricing optimization or better visibility.
